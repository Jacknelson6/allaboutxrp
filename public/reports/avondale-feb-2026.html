Content Performance

What's working right now

Two content formats are consistently outperforming everything else. These should be the foundation of every shoot going forward.

Top Performer

Talking-Head Videos

Allison and John on camera sharing lending insights, market takes, and borrower education. These build trust fast. The audience wants to hear from real people, not graphics.

Top Performer

House Tours

Walking through properties and calling out cool features, unique builds, renovation potential. These perform because they're aspirational and tangible. People can picture themselves in the deal.

Everything else (static graphics, market stats carousels, generic tips) is underperforming relative to these two. The recommendation is to double down on what's working and phase out what isn't.

Strategy

Organic-first approach for Q1

With paid on pause, organic is the entire growth engine. The good news: 65K views and 553 new follows in half a month proves the content is reaching people. The focus now is converting that attention into action.

01

Hook-First Editing

First 1-2 seconds need to stop the scroll. Open with the most interesting part of the house or the boldest take from the interview.

02

CTA in Every Post

Facebook has 387 new follows and zero clicks. Every post needs a clear next step, whether it's "link in bio" or "DM us for rates."

03

Repurpose Long-Form

YouTube interviews should be chopped into 5-8 short clips per video. One shoot fuels weeks of content across every platform.

March 5, 2026 — 11am to 2pm CST

Next Shoot Game Plan

  • 2-3 Talking-Head Segments
    Allison and/or John on camera. Topics: current lending market outlook, what borrowers are getting wrong, why private lending over traditional banks. Keep each segment 3-5 min, edit into short-form clips.
  • 1-2 Property Tours
    Walk through active deals or recently funded properties. Call out features that make it a good investment. Show the "before" if it's a renovation project.
  • Quick-Hit Q&A Clips
    Rapid-fire questions Allison answers in 15-30 seconds each. "What's the minimum credit score?" "How fast can you close?" These are SEO gold and great for Reels/Shorts.
  • B-Roll Package
    Office shots, handshake moments, property exteriors, paperwork signing. Gives the editing team filler footage for months of content.
